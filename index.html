Warehouse management system

Prime WMS is a warehouse management system is a cloud based SaaS solution that offers visibility into a business’ entire inventory and manages the supply chain from fulfilment operations to the market store shelf. Prime WMS helps a warehouse operation improve their space utilization by optimizing resource usage and material flows, thereby increasing overall efficiency and hence a reduction in cost of operation. Being a cloud based solution, Prime WMS allows for an organization to quickly get online and get operational. Unlike a traditional on premise software solution, all upgrades are seamless. Prime WMS is a SaaS application and hence the total cost of ownership is low and entry cost is low as well. Prime WMS is brought to you by Southshore Innovations Private Limited, a technology focused company based in Chennai.

About Us

Southshore Innovations Private Limited is focused on bringing technology to the field of publishing and its distribution & logistics. With this as a core, we have looked at the traditional publishing market as it exists today, the number of small publishers, the fragmentation in the market in India and have tailored products/ solutions which would help this segment become more efficient and give them the ability to scale.
