About Us

Southshore Innovations Private Limited is an organisation based in Chennai and focused on cloud based SaaS solutions for different segments of the publishing industry in India.

The publishing Industry while large and diverse in India is very fragmented and till recently technology has not been utilised by this industry especially in the sales / marketing / distribution side of the business.

With this as our focus, Southshore is working on different solutions for publishers, booksellers and logistics operators in this publishing market. Prime WMS is one such solution which is adaptable for not just the publishing industry but across any organisation small or large which handles inventory.
